Let's compare the use allogeneic to autologous cells in funded research, clinical trials, and patents.

First, some datasets are easier to download than query.

Go to NSF Award Search, search for 'mesenchymal', and download results
Save .csv to user_provided/nsf_awards
https://nsf.gov/awardsearch/simpleSearchResult?queryText=mesenchymal&ActiveAwards=true&ExpiredAwards=true

Go to NIH Clinical Trials, search for 'mesenchymal' in the intervention field,  and download results
Save .csv to user_provided/nih_clinicaltrials
https://clinicaltrials.gov/ct2/results?cond=&term=&type=&rslt=&age_v=&gndr=&intr=mesenchymal&titles=&outc=&spons=&lead=&id=&cntry=&state=&city=&dist=&locn=&rsub=&strd_s=&strd_e=&prcd_s=&prcd_e=&sfpd_s=&sfpd_e=&rfpd_s=&rfpd_e=&lupd_s=&lupd_e=&sort=

Go to NIH Reporter and download results
Save .csv to user_provided/nih_reporter
* can only save 1500 results at a time, search for 'mesenchymal', so download a year a time
https://reporter.nih.gov/search/yPC8W3c0jEWB7BkBQlpR_Q/projects
